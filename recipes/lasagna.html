Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Masala
  • Bread
  • chesse

Steps

  1. Brown ground meat (beef, pork, or a combination) in a large skillet or Dutch oven.
  2. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add chopped onions, garlic, and other vegetables like carrots or bell peppers, and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and/or tomato sauce.
  5. Season with Italian herbs (like oregano, basil, parsley), salt, pepper, and optionally a pinch of sugar to balance acidity.
  6. Simmer the sauce for at least 30 minutes, or longer for a richer flavor, stirring occasionally.
